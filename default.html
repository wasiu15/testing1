Feel Confident With VENUS

Fashion is about more than just the clothes you wear from day-to-day. Fashion is self-expression that allows you to tell the world who you are and what you stand for. More importantly, what you wear can affect how you feel about yourself, so it’s important to wear something that you absolutely love.

You want to look your best, and the women’s fashion options from VENUS are a great place to start.

Unique Clothing for Your Everyday Life

No matter what your personal style is, there are flattering outfits that you’re sure to feel good in. Go for a comfortable, classy jumpsuit or a classic professional pantsuit to wear to the office. When the weekend rolls around, go for some more casual looks with jeans, rompers, shorts, skirts, dresses, and a variety of different tops.

When it’s cold outside, you can find coats and jackets ranging from hoodies and bomber jackets to trench coats and faux fur. Cardigans and sweaters are also an option, offering off-the-shoulder designs, lacing, crochet, and eye-catching cutouts.

Loungewear and activewear, like hoodies and tracksuits, are great for comfy days in or for days when you’re headed out for a jog, to the gym, or to run some weekend errands. You can get even more comfortable with cute and cozy pajama sets and nightgowns to wear to bed.

Accessories and Jewelry

A great outfit isn’t just about the clothes, though. You can take your outfit up a notch with stylish accessories to tie it all together. Belts allow you to take command of your silhouette and alter the shape of your dresses and tops by cinching in the waist, hip, or underbust. They can also add an accent color to an outfit that you can then match with a handbag, tote or clutch. Throw on a pair of cool shades and a hat in the summer or a scarf and gloves to keep warm in the winter to complete the look.

You can dress up any outfit with unique jewelry pieces. From fringe earrings in bright, funky colors to chunky bangles and stylish chokers, there’s plenty of fashionable pieces to make your outfit 100% your own. Finish your look off with a comfortable pair of boots or wedges or a sexy pair of heels.

Fun and Functional Swimwear and Beachwear

Just because you’re on vacation doesn’t mean you can’t still dress to impress. You can build cute casual outfits to wear to the beach or the pool with fun swimwear and beachwear. Build the base of your outfit with a classic one-piece or show some skin with a sexy monokini. If you’re more comfortable in a two-piece, you can mix and match your tops and bottoms for a truly customized look. Choose from a halter top, a bandeau, or a push-up bikini top and then pair it with some cheeky bottoms or a high-waisted bottom – whatever makes you feel most comfortable. Add a lightweight cover-up for some shopping or dining on your vacation, whether you prefer a long, flowy dress, a kimono top, or a romper or jumpsuit.

Look Good Under Your Clothes With Lingerie

A cute bra and a good pair of undies can boost your confidence and make you feel sexy and empowered. Whether you prefer the support of a structured push-up or the freedom of a comfy bralette, there’s a perfect bra out there for you. You can even show it off a little with a plunging neckline or a backless dress.

Everyone’s got their favorite style of underwear. Bikini, thong, hipster, and boyshort styles are all fabulous choices offered in lace, nylon, spandex and cotton blends for a snug but comfortable fit. The right underwear can help your whole outfit look better too. Shapewear allows you to perfect your silhouette and accentuate your curves in all the right places. It can smooth out the look of tight-fitting dresses.

Not sure about sizing? No worries. We’ve got a helpful lingerie sizing guide to help you find the perfect fit for your body.

Try Out Trending Styles

Keeping up with all of the trends that hit women’s clothing stores can be intimidating, but we can help. Take a look at our trends page, where you can get updates on what’s in and now. You can quickly and easily find clothing pieces that are in this season, so you can keep up-to-date without any effort.

Online shopping for women makes fashion easy. Check out the trendy, stylish pieces here on VENUS, and find clothes that you truly love.

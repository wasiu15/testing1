Pre-order items are new items that have not yet arrived in our warehouse. When you select your desired color and size, the item page will display the expected date the merchandise will be available. Please note that the date provided is tentative and is not guaranteed. You will not be charged for pre-order items until they are ready for shipment and you may cancel your pre-order purchase at any time before the item is processed.

A backorder is an order placed for an out-of-stock item before it is restocked. Only certain items are available for backorder. When you select your desired color and size, the item page will display the expected date the backordered item is expected to be restocked. Please note that the date provided is tentative and is not guaranteed. If there is a delay in the delivery of the merchandise to our warehouse, you will be notified by email. You will not be charged for backorder items until they are ready for shipment and you may cancel your backorder purchase at any time before the item is processed.

There are two reasons why your backorder may have been cancelled:

Were unable to process payment: Since we do not collect funds for items that are not in stock, you are not charged up-front for backorder items. The payment is processed only when the item is ready for shipment. If your card expired in the time since you originally placed the order or if the funds are not available, your order may be cancelled.

Were unable to accept the delivery of merchandise from the vendor: If the merchandise does not meet our quality standards, it will be returned to the vendor. In this event, we would no longer have an expected date of delivery and all orders for the item are cancelled. In this case you will receive a notification via email.

If you have questions regarding a backorder cancellation, please contact our Customer Service team and well be happy to assist you.

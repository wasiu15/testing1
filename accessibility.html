Accessibility Statement

Venus Fashion, Inc. (Venus) is committed to making its website usable by all people, inlcuding those with disabilities by meeting or exceeding the requirements of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.1 Level A/AA (WCAG 2.1 A/AA). We strive to make our site an equal experience for everyone.

We continue to monitor our site and make sure that all content provided is accessible to all visitors. In order to help us in this process, we have engaged The Bureau of Internet Accessibility, an accessibilty consulting company, to conduct a full accessibility audit consisting of both automated and manual testing of our website using BolA's A11y® platform which provides us with specific recommendations that will assist us in ensuring that our Web presence is in conformance with the WCAG 2.1 A/AA.

DISCLAIMER

It is important to note that efforts to the website are ongoing as we work to implement the relevant improvements to meet WCAG 2.1 A/AA guidelines over time.

ACCESSIBILITY SUPPORT

If you are having any issues relating to assistive technology, including screen readers, keyboard access, or other issues relating to the accessibility of our website, we want to help you achieve what you came to do. A live agent is standing by, ready to take your toll-free call and help resolve your issue together. Call 833-371-0345 to talk now.

If you prefer to provide feedback by email, please write to venus@a11y.com and be sure to specify the webpage so we can look into making the page accessible for you. We gladly value your questions and comments.

THIRD PARTY SITES

While you are visiting our site, you will notice that we sometimes link to third-party sites such as Twitter, Instagram, Pinterest, YouTube, and Facebook to provide information about Venus. Venus does not control these sites and cannot guarantee their accessibility. Please contact these third parties directly if you have difficulties accessing their content.

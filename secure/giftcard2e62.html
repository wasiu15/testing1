Help
online

When you arrive at the Billing and Shipping page of checkout, you will notice a place to enter up to three gift cards toward one purchase.

If you have more you’d like to apply to a single order, call 1 (888) 782-2224, and a Venus account specialist will help you.

If your purchase is more than the amount on your gift card(s), a credit card is required and the balance will be placed on your credit card. If your purchase, including shipping, handling and applicable taxes, is less than the amount on your purchase, the balance will remain on the gift card.

by phone

Call 1 (888) 782-2224 Monday through Friday, 7:00AM EST - 1:00AM EST. With your gift card at hand, you then can give the numbers highlighted above to the Venus account specialist.

lost or stolen

If this card is lost, stolen, or destroyed, Venus may replace it if you provide the original receipt or card and pin number. Replacement card will be the value of the card at the time Venus receives your report of loss, theft or unauthorized use.

cash redemption

Your card is redeemable for cash when the balance is less than $5 or as otherwise required by law. We do not charge sales tax on the purchase of gift cards.

expiration

This gift card does not expire. Cardholders may take their time and select the apparel most pleasing for them season after season.

balance inquiry

For balance inquiry, call 1 (888) 782-2224, or check online.

gift cards and discount offers

Special offers or discounts cannot be applied to the purchase of gift cards. However, when a gift card is redeemed, it can be combined with a special offer or discount offer and used to pay for items. If you purchase both gift cards and items online and enter a promo code, the promo will be applied only to the merchandise in your order.

your gift recipient's guarantee

We are only happy when you are. This guarantee extends to your gift recipient whom we are positive will be thrilled with the gift she is about to receive. We are so positive that if she is ever unsatisfied with a Venus purchase, she may return it within 90 days for a prompt exchange or refund – her choice. Please note that any refund would be given to her in the form of another gift card – since that would be her original method of payment.

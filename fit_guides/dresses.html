VENUS Dress Fit Guide

VENUS knows our customers are as unique and trendy as our selection of women's fashion. So, when you're looking for your next new outfit, let our fashion fit experts help you find the styles most suited to flatter your body type!

Finding a style that fits you best starts with your needs based on how you feel about different areas of your body. Sometimes we want to accentuate certain areas, and sometimes we want to minimize our least favorite ones! From trendy maxi dresses to that chic little black dress, whatever your specific needs, we can help you find a look that helps you stand out while feeling beautiful, comfortable, and confident.

This VENUS Dress Fit Guide provides helpful tips to identify a variety of dress styles that work best with certain body types. Whether you're looking for styles to suit your height, curves, or size, we'll help you find the perfect fit just for YOU. Every woman deserves to look as unique and beautiful as she feels, so embrace your body and use our dress fit guide to find the perfect look for any occasion!

Let’s begin! What part of the body would you like to focus on?

You are thin, with a straight waist and narrow shoulders and hips. This body type will benefit from adding definition to the waistline. Look for dresses with details at the bust and hips. Puff or cap sleeves, full skirts, ruching at the hips, and belts at waist give the appearance of a curvier figure.

Your bust size is fuller than your hips and rear. Look for wrap styles, classic V-necks and halters that will draw eyes downward and create a slimming effect. Keep top embellishments to a minimum. To help balance out your bottom half, choose full skirts and A-line styles.

You have a thin waist, narrow ribs and shoulders, and full, womanly hips. The waistline should be accentuated. Look for dresses with wide collars and details around the bust to balance your lower body. A-line and flared skirts, empire and fitted waists, and belts worn at waistline are best for you.

You have broad shoulders and/or a voluptuous chest, a smaller waist and gorgeous full hips. Look for dresses that accentuate your curves and have a fitted waist. Fitted clothing, strait skirts and belted waists are best for you.

You have thinner legs and arms, accompanied by fullness through the ribs and stomach. Look for dresses that accentuates your legs and arms. Loose-fitting waists, empire waists, short sleeves, full skirts, and knee-length skirts are best for you.

You are smaller than the average woman. Look for clothing that lengthens your silhouette. Dresses with a hemline just above the knee are elongating. Vertical stripes, solid colors, fitted clothing and empire waists are best for you.
